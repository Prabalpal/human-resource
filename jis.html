information about University

Published on December 8, 2023

JIS University, Kolkata is ranked 1st in the category of Multidisciplinary University (Overall Rank 76 in India) and Private & Deemed Multidisciplinary Category (Overall Rank 38) in The Week - Hansa Research Survey 2020. JIS University offers undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma courses in the specialized fields of Science, Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy, Management, Law, Social Sciences and Education. Admission to the JIS University done on the basis of merit, entrance examination and cutoff criteria of the institute. The University provides PhD programs in various courses as well. The University always encourages entrepreneurship and skill development among the students for promoting entrepreneurship, employability and self-employment. Additionally, the university has a training and placement cell providing placement assistance to its students. The highest package recorded at INR 4 LPA and the average package recorded at INR 2.11 LPA. Some of the potential employers of JISU Kolkata are CS, Wipro, mPhasis, GSK, Alembic, Artech Infosystems, Palsons Derma, Nestle, Cipla, Epsilon Learning, 3rd Life, KGS Technologies, Just Dial, Nerdy Turtlez, Extramarks, Indusnet Technologies, Cogito software, etc.

JIS medical school

Published on December 7, 2023

Chief Minister of West Bengal Mamata Banerjee Officially Inaugurates the JIS School of Medical Science & Research The JIS School of Medical Science & Research was inaugurated by Mamata Banerjee, the Hon’ble Chief Minister of West Bengal, remotely on July 24, 2023. JIS Group in collaboration with the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Government of West Bengal has founded the JIS School of Medical Science & Research in Santragachi, Howrah in Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode. With approval from the National Medical Council, the institute seeks to offer admission to 150 students annually to its prestigious MBBS course effective from the academic year 2023-24. Sardar Taranjit Singh, the Managing Director of JIS Group, along with the members of the Governing Board, attended the event. The JIS Group signed an MOU at the Bengal Global Business Summit with the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Govt. of West Bengal, to establish the JIS School of Medical Science & Research. JIS Group is committed to cater to healthcare and medical education in West Bengal. Moreover, the JIS management envisions the JIS School of Medical Science & Research facility evolving into a state-of-the-art 1200-bed hospital spread across 30 acres dedicated to serving the healthcare needs of the community.
